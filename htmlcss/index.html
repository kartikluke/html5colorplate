Hello there, welcome to Colorplate!

Say hello to the world's only mood & colour based framework generator based on the famous HTML5 boilerplate and our very own color based templating algorithm! Powered by Java, LESScompile, Boilerplate and Adobe Kuler's API, Colorplate hopes to give the world a free framework generator based on themed colors. Stick around for the ride!



Get it now!


It's Smart

Powered by some neat erm..powering stuff including our very own ColorSorting algorithm! Enter a theme (For ex, Superman) and watch our algorithm use Kuler's themesorting to find the best colorcombo for your string and turn it into HTML and CSS with the right colors in the right place! Search for popular and random themes too! Even the buttons used some really cool hex algebra to generate gradients and shadows! A perfect fix for your mood.

It's Fast

Lightweight and ready to go out of the box, the short executable does what it needs to generate a blazing fast framework with a theme based on your mood. It boils down to a bare 65kb (23kb minified), that's ridiculously fast! It's also XTHML and CSS3 valid. Go ahead, try it!

It's Moody

Want to find the best themes for the month? Random ones? Want the background and foreground in your theme to switch? Yeah - Colorplate does that shizz. It's also got some essential basic bootstrapping functionality - we don't include the barrage of weaponry that Twitter Bootstrap or Foundation gives you, but we've got the essentials. Get straight to the thick of things!
